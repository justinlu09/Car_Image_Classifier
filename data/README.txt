The images are from the Stanford Car Dataset found in the link below. The images have been sorted into their respective categories. Note that the cars_train and cars_test folders do not contain all the images (GitHub limits to 1000). Please download the devkit in the link listed below. A script will be provided to organize images into their respective categories.
https://ai.stanford.edu/~jkrause/cars/car_dataset.html

Citation:
3D Object Representations for Fine-Grained Categorization
Jonathan Krause, Michael Stark, Jia Deng, Li Fei-Fei
4th IEEE Workshop on 3D Representation and Recognition, at ICCV 2013 (3dRR-13). Sydney, Australia. Dec. 8, 2013.
